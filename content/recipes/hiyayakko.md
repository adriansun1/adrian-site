## Ingredients
- 2tsp Ginger, grated
- 2tbsp Soy Sauce/ Mentsuyu/ Ponzu
- Silken tofu
- Katsuoboshi
- Scallion
- Cucumber
- Wakame
- Corn
- Sesame Seeds 

## Directions
- drain tofu
- add any toppings in a tasteful manner
- pour sauce over top

NOTES: 
all ingredients optional except tofu of course.
